Who we are ?

A Learning Management System – also known as an E-guru platform - is a cloud-based software package that enables platform to deliver learning content and resources to learners. This E-guru platform is often web-and cloud-based to facilitate 24/7 access to e-learning courses and relevant education. Furthermore, an E-guru Platform is also called "Course Management System", "Pedagogical Platform" or "e-Learning Management System".

E-guru Platforms are considered to be the missing bridge between current education improvements and effective uses of technology. Typically, an elearning platform encourages instructors to guide and manage learner achievement more effectively by contextualizing the learning experience itself in a consistent and creative way. Learning companies agree that technology alone will not make significant changes to their company. Rather, technology must be comprehensively and consistently integrated in order to transform the way people – more specifically students –.
